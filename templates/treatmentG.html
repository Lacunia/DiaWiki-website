Gestational Diabetes Treatments

1. Change your lifestyle

  • For gestational diabetes patients, you are currently pregnant.
  • A healthy diet and regular, moderate exercises can be the first option for you in order to treat diabetes.

2. Insulin intake / medication

  • If your blood sugar level continues to rise, your doctor may prescribe medications or insulins for you.
