Treatments

No matter what types of diabetes you have, your primary objective when treating them is to keep your blood sugar level in check. Please note that below are just some general treatments for each type. Ultimately, it is still best to consult your family doctor or a walk-in doctor to receive a more personalized treatment.

Type I Treatments

As a type 1 diabetes patient, taking insulin shot everyday is one types of treatment.

Type II Treatments

As a type 2 diabetes patient, fitness and healthy diet will help you manage your diabetes.

Gestational Diabetes Treatments

As a gestational diabetes patient, meal plans and scheduled physical activity will help keep your blood glucose levels equal to those of healthy pregnant women.
