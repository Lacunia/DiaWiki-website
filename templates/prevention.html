Preventions

This page will inform you of various ways that you can use to prevent yourself from getting diabetes.

01

Lose weight

  • Losing weight can be an effective way of preventing diabetes. By losing approximately 7% to 10% of your body weight once you are diagnosed with prediabetes, which means that you have a high risk of contracting diabetes, is said to be able to reduce your risk by almost 60%.
  • However, be cautious not to lose too much weight, lest it affect your health negatively. Your goal is to first meet your weight loss goal of around 7 to 10%, and then maintain a healthy weight moving forward.

02

Do regular/daily exercises

  • Regular physical activities and exercises can help reduce blood sugar and also increase your bodyâ€™s sensitivity to insulin, preventing your cells from not responding to it.
  • From personal experiences of prediabetes patients, doing 30 min of aerobic exercises after breakfast, lunch, and dinner can really help keep your blood sugar pressure in check.

03

Consume healthy plant food that contains fiber

  • Fiber-rich food can absorb sugar inside your body, lowering your blood sugar level, and help you lose weight.
  • examples of fiber-rich food:
    • Fruits, such as tomatoes, peppers, etc.
    • Non starchy vegetables, such as leafy greens, broccoli, etc.
    • Legumes, such as beans, chickpeas, etc.
    • Whole grains, such as whole-wheat bread
  • Be creative and you can create healthy but also delicious food with these ingredients.

04

Avoid food with high sugar content

  • Since the core problem of diabetes is the high blood sugar level, intaking even more sugar and not enough fiber can increase your chance of getting diabetes.
  • examples of food to avoid:
    • White bread, pastries, and flours
    • Fruits that contain a huge amount of sugar
    • Processed food with sugar or high-fructose corn syrup
  • Check out our forum to find posts about processed foods that are safe for you to consume.
